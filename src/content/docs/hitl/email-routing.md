---
title: Email Approval Routing
description: Route pending actions to email for approval.
---

Email routing belongs in the same defer/approval family as Slack routing. The runtime already supports notification channels for deferred work, and email is the obvious low-friction channel for slow review loops.

Use email when you need an auditable human workflow that does not depend on someone watching a live dashboard.

See [Human-in-the-Loop Overview](/hitl/overview/) and [Approval Workflows](/hitl/approval-workflows/).
