Nexus is the enterprise deployment story: keep the control plane inside your own boundary while still using the same governance model, audit trail, and integration surfaces.

The public core repository does not contain a Nexus-specific implementation tree, so this page stays at the product boundary level instead of inventing internal modules. The key promise is the same: the AAB, the policy engine, the Audit Ledger, and the HITL flow remain intact inside your network.

See [Self-Hosted Deployment](/deployment/self-hosted/) and [Threat Model](/security/threat-model/).
